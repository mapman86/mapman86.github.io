Who I am

I am a geographer with a passion for creating geographic visualizations, or maps, that solve complex problems. My greatest interest is in finding solutions to medical and public health issues through geographic analysis of environmental and public health data. View my site Public Health Geographics to learn more about my work in medical geography. I am currently pursuing a PhD in geography and a graduate certificate in public health. Why? Because I believe that geospatial analysis is underutilized in pubic health and I want to be a well trained leader in the field.
