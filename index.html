Who I am

I am currently a PhD candidate in Medical Geography at the University of Utah and the Director of the Office of Primary Care and Rural Health at the Utah Department of Health. I have over ten years of experience in GIS and spatial analysis. I am interested in population health, community health needs assessments, health disparities and social determinants of health. I am an out-of-the-box thinker and problem solver who loves to collaborate, build strong relationships and turn great ideas into reality. . My greatest interest is in finding solutions to medical and public health issues through geographic analysis of environmental and public health data. View my site Public Health Geographics to learn more about my work in medical geography.
