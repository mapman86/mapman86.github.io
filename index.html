Maps and data
about all things related to public health.

Used by researchers and public health professionals

Health Maps is a resource for academic researchers and public health professionals who are looking for health related data and maps. The major contribution of this site is the GIS data depot where researchers and public health professionals can download popular health indicator data in GIS format to use in mapping and spatial analysis. All of the data, maps, methods and research published on this site is free and open to the public.

This site is developed and maintained by Matt McCullough, a PhD student at the University of Utah. Connect via LinkedIn
